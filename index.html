About the Simulation

TRA 3440 — Introduction to Air Transportation

University of West Florida · Summer 2026 · Dr. Kenny O'Connor

This simulation places you as the CEO of a regional airline operating out of Pensacola (PNS) with routes to Atlanta (ATL), Orlando (MCO), and Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW). Each round you set fares, allocate 30 flights across the three routes, choose a passenger-cargo mix, and invest in Market Development and Operational Capability.

The simulation runs for 5 rounds, each with a different market scenario. Your cumulative profit across all rounds determines your final ranking.
