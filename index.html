About the Simulation

Overview

You are the CEO of a startup regional airline operating out of Pensacola (PNS) with routes to Atlanta (ATL), Orlando (MCO), and Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW). Each round, you set fares, allocate flights, choose your passenger/cargo mix, and invest in market development and operational capability.

Your goal: maximize cumulative profit over five rounds. Market conditions change each round — what works in Round 1 may not work in Round 4. Adapt or fall behind.
